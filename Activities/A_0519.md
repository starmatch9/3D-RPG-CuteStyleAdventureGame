# 5.19
## Activity 1
#### Title: Yuka's Adventure

Idea: A 3D adventure game that is as fun, cheerful, cute and hilarious as Candyman.

Repeated actions: character movement, collecting gold coins, collision detection, etc.

Unity format: 3D free perspective.

Core system: 3C system, including the movement, jumping, and gliding of characters.

Github: https://github.com/starmatch9/3D-RPG-CuteStyleAdventureGame

Biggest risk: Gliding + jumping in 3D free perspective requires tight, predictable response. If movement is slippery or landing feels bad, "fun & cute" instantly dies. No art can save bad 3C.

MVP: Single meadow scene. Player runs, jumps, glides. Collect 5–10 coins. One silly gag.
